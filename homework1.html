Veg Biryani

Today i am going to tell you the reciepe of my favourite food i.e.veg biryani

  • INGREDIENTS
    • 1 cup basmati rice
    • 1 medium onion sliced
    • 1 green chili
    • 1 teaspoon ginger gerlic paste,2 teaspoon mint leaves,2 teaspoon coriander leaves
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala, red chilli and 1 small tomato
    • 2 teaspoons of oiL AND curd
    • 2 cups water and 1/8 teaspoon salt for veggies
    • 1 cup green vegetables

    STEPS TO PREPARE THE DISH

    1. Add oil to the pressure cooker or pot and heat it
    2. add all the whole spices for vegetable biryani
    3. saute for 2 min and add chopped onions and green chilli
    4. next add ginger garlic paste and saute untill the raw smell has gone off
    5. add tomatoes,sal,curd,mint and coriander leaves and garam masala
    6. drain the water and add it in layers evenly over the veggies
    7. add salt and lemon juice . miss well and taste the water add more if needed and pour the salted water acrross the sidesof the pot
    8. For pressure cooking: cover the cooker and pressure cook for 1 whistle on a medium high flame. turn off the stove. for non mushy veg biriyani release the pressure after 1 to 2 mins. When the presure goes down ,open the lid and fluff up vef biryani with a fork

    Your veg biryani is ready to serve.
